# Ferrari SF90 Stradale - Hybrid Hypercar Engineering

**Target Platform**: linkedin  
**Duration**: 60  
**Audience Level**: intermediate

## Key Points
1. The SF90 combines a 4.0L twin-turbo V8 (780 HP) with three electric motors (220 HP total) for 986 HP combined output
2. First production Ferrari with AWD - two front electric motors provide torque vectoring and instant traction
3. Achieves 0-100 km/h in 2.5 seconds using pre-boost strategy where electric motors spool up the turbos before acceleration
